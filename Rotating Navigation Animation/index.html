Oppenheimer

"Oppenheimer" is a compelling historical drama centered on the life of J. Robert Oppenheimer, the renowned physicist played brilliantly by Oscar-winning actor, Daniel Day-Lewis. Directed by the visionary filmmaker, Christopher Nolan, the film navigates Oppenheimer's journey through scientific brilliance, personal conflicts, and the moral implications of creating the atomic bomb. Supported by a talented cast, including Cate Blanchett as his confidante and Tom Hardy as a fellow scientist, the movie skillfully captures the tumultuous era that shaped the course of human history.
